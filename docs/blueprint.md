# **App Name**: DailyPulse

## Core Features:

- Daily Mood Check-in: Allow users to select their current mood from a predefined list (Happy, Calm, Stressed, Sad, Angry, Motivated, Tired) using intuitive emoji or icon selection.
- Generative Mood Guide: After a mood selection, an AI tool will generate a short, supportive message and 1-2 practical suggestions relevant to the chosen mood, ensuring variety and personalization.
- Mood Data Persistence: Store the selected mood along with the date in the PostgreSQL database, ensuring users can only submit one entry per day or edit their existing daily entry.
- Mood History Tracker: Display a clear view of past mood selections through a simple list or basic chart, enabling users to identify and track personal mood patterns over time.
- Daily Check-in Reminder: Implement a subtle UI notification system to prompt users for their daily mood check-in, encouraging consistent engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color, `#4D5EA3`, is a serene, muted blue-violet. This choice evokes a sense of calm and thoughtfulness, ideal for a mental wellness application, while retaining sufficient depth for readability against a lighter background.
- The background color, `#F0F2F9`, is a very light, almost white, tone with a subtle hint of the primary hue. This keeps the interface clean and spacious, promoting a feeling of clarity and minimizing visual clutter, consistent with a minimal design aesthetic.
- An accent color of `#6FC0EA`, a brighter and slightly more saturated blue-cyan, provides a contrasting element for interactive components and highlights. This analogous choice creates a harmonious palette while ensuring important actions stand out.
- The 'Inter' sans-serif typeface is chosen for both headlines and body text. Its clean, modern, and objective characteristics support the requested minimal design, while its humanist qualities ensure a friendly and approachable tone that avoids feeling clinical.
- For mood selection, use expressive and clear emojis or simple, modern line-art icons to enhance user understanding and interaction. General interface icons should maintain a consistent, minimal line-art style.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout with generous whitespace to maintain a minimal aesthetic. The design prioritizes content readability and will be fully mobile-responsive, adapting seamlessly across different device sizes to ensure accessibility on the go.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transition animations for UI elements, such as mood selection feedback or new content loading, to enhance the user experience without being distracting, reinforcing the app's gentle and supportive tone.
